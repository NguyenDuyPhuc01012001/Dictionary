cá
* noun
     - Click, pawl
     - Steel tip, toe plate
     - Fish
          Ex: cá nước ngọt => fresh water fish
          Ex: câu cá => to fish
          Ex: ao sâu tốt cá => in the deepest water is the best fishing
          Ex: Cá Tuế => Minnow
          Ex: Cá Betta
     - (particle denoting fish-like animals living in water)
          Ex: cá sấu => a crocodile
          Ex: cá voi => a whale

dog
